toki pona ku (e nimi ilo)

This is a master list of all the unique words in the toki pona language that includes new words from lipu ku (The Toki Pona Dictionary) and a few helpful combination words compiled from the dictionary section in lipu pu (Toki Pona: The Language of Good) merged with some other dictionaries I found online.

For reference, here are the other dictionaries I merged with lipu pu:

  • http://tokipona.net/tp/ClassicWordList.aspx
  • https://devurandom.xyz/tokipona/dictionary.html
  • https://devurandom.xyz/tokipona/13.html
  • https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Toki_Pona_lemmas

I also pulled a couple definitions from jan Misali's video on ku: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n3p4-GFXrkM

You can find an Anki deck of this list for flashcard practice here on AnkiWeb: https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/1075479788.

num. two
n. duo, pair
adj. divided
v. double, divide

Using “tu” at the end of a noun phrase is generally associated with the number 2. The meaning “divided” is usually specified by using a “li” particle:

  • kulupu tu = two communities
  • kulupu li tu. = the community is divided.

v. to unite

Using “wan” at the end of a noun phrase is generally associated with the number 1. The meaning “united” is usually specified by using a “li” particle:

  • kulupu wan = one community
  • kulupu li wan. = the community is united.
  • kulupu mute wan = 21 communities (complex numbering system)
  • kulupu mute li wan = many (or 20) communities are united.
